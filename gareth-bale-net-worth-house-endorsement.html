Following that contract, he is likely to receive a yearly income of  $33 million through salary and bonuses.

Career

Bale started his club career as a full back in Southampton. He made his club debut against Millwall on 17 April 2006.

In Tottenham, due to his pace and strength, he was deployed as a winger, and within few years, he turned into a world-class player.

Similarly, he has made 533 appearances at the club level scoring 181 goals. Furthermore, he made 45 appearances for Southhampton, scoring 5 goals.

Afterward, he moved to Tottenham in 2007 and remained there until 2013. Between that years, He played for Tottenham 203 times, scoring 55 goals

Similarly, in 2021 he again moved to Tottenham as a loan, where he made 34 appearances and scored 16 goals.

In 2013 he moved to Real Madrid for a record price. He performed excellently for Real Madrid, scoring 105 goals in 251 appearances.

Similarly, he made his international debut for Wales in 2006. As of now, he has made 90 appearances for his country and has scored 33 goals.

In fact, he is the highest goal scorer for his country.

Throughout his career, he has won many trophies like Laliga (2106-17, 2019-20), Copa Del Ray (2016-17), Supercopa(2017).

Moreover, he is a four-time champion league winner (2013-14, 2015-16, 2016-17, and 2017-18). He has also won UEFA Super Cup (2014,2017) and the FIFA Club World cup(2014, 2017, and 2018).
